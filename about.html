About

Andrew Oh

I am currently a senior at Michigan State University studying to become a 3d artist with experience in multiple graphic design, 3d modeling and video editing programs which includes:

  • Blender
  • Adobe CC
  • Maya 2020
  • Unreal Engine
  • Cascadeur
  • Zbrush

I've spent countless hours in these various applications and enjoy challenging myself with intricate projects that encompass architectural design, character modeling, and product visualization. My enjoyment of 3D modeling has been a driving force in my life, and I'm determined to turn my passion into a full-fledged career. Many of my interests have also lead me to explore audio production and video editing which I have made into a side hustle selling custom made sounds for music producers and video editors.
